An Angel's Friendship


Just eight months since we met,
Yet it feels like forever, I bet.
You're beautiful, like an angel so true,
Sent by God to help me through.

In bad times, you stand by my side,
In good times, we laugh and glide.
People say boy and girl can only be two,
Brother-sister or lovers, but we prove it's untrue.

This friendship we share, so rare, so fine,
Buddy, you're a blessing, a star that shines.
Happy birthday, and thank you, dear friend,
For making my life better, on you I depend.

In a world so vast, with many around,

Melo, my best buddy, it's clear to see,
You're not just a friend, you're family to me.

Through laughs and tears, joy and strife,
You bring so much light to my life.
Happy birthday, my dear friend,
With you, my joy will never end.
